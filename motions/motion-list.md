# Motion List

Practice motions tagged by **type** and **domain**, so Lucas never burns prep time
hunting for one. Feeds two things:
- **The 90-second Type Drill** (use the pre-mixed sets at the bottom).
- **The daily speed-writes** (pick from the week's domain; start Foundation, climb to Stretch).

**Type key:** Pol = Policy · Abo = Abolition · Val = Value/Analysis · Reg = Regret ·
Cmp = Comparative · Stn = Stance · Act = Actor · Bal = Balance
**Difficulty:** 🟢 Foundation · 🟡 Core · 🔴 Stretch

## How to pick for speed-writes
- **Match the week's domain** (table headers show which week).
- **Climb the difficulty:** 🟢 early in the week, 🔴 by Thursday.
- **Alternate sides:** prep Prop one day, Opp the next on a similar motion — clash comes
  from knowing both.
- **Need more?** Ask the `debate-coach` agent to generate a fresh batch tagged in this
  format, or recombine: any `[actor]`, `[ban X]`, or `[A over B]` frame becomes a new
  motion.
