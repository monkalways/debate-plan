> ## Your two substantives to BUILD (1st Opp)
>
> **① RETRIBUTION / JUSTICE IS OWED (the principle):**
> - CLAIM: a serious wrong deserves proportionate punishment *for its own sake*, independent of any future benefit.
> - CHAIN: crime is a moral wrong against a victim + society → justice requires the wrong be *answered* proportionally → strip that out and you deny the victim's dignity and the moral order, and public faith in justice collapses (→ people take it into their own hands).
> - IMPACT: victims' dignity + social trust in the system.
> - WEIGH: even if rehab "worked," sending a murderer to a wellness program *instead of* punishment isn't justice — desert is prior to outcomes.
>
> **② DETERRENCE + INCAPACITATION / PROTECT FUTURE VICTIMS (the practical):**
> - CLAIM: punishment protects the public *with certainty*; rehab-first *gambles* safety on uncertain reform.
> - CHAIN: real consequences deter would-be offenders (soften them → weaker deterrent → more crime) AND incapacitation removes dangerous people *now* (certain, present protection) → rehab is uncertain, slow, and some won't reform → prioritising it means releasing/under-punishing on a hope → present victims pay the bill.
> - IMPACT: future victims — protected by certainty, not a gamble.
> - WEIGH: the *certainty* of protection + owed justice outweighs the *hope* of reform.
>

> and **owed** justice for an **uncertain** future gain. *(Cross-link your bank: [[mechanism-bank#Deterrence]],
