# Progress Log

Track every recorded/timed speech here. The point is to measure **skill, not activity** —
watch the trend on the two weakness lines (Knowledge, Style) and the ceiling line
(**Weighing**), not just the prep clock.

## How to score
After each speech, rate 1–10 on the six rubric dimensions. Be honest — a flattering log
is useless. For **Weigh** and **Clash**, get a *human* check where possible (coach Fri,
peer recording-swap Sat); self-review is reliable mainly for Style and Speed/delivery.

| Dimension | Quick test |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Know** (Knowledge & depth) | Did I use a real example/mechanism, not just assertion? |
| **Struct** (Structure & logic) | Did the 4-link chains hold? Was it signposted? |
| **Weigh** (Comparative) | Did I say *why my side outweighs* (scope/probability/magnitude/reversibility/timeframe)? |
| **Clash** (Responsiveness) | Did I engage their actual argument, not a strawman? POIs handled? |
| **Style** (Fluency) | Vivid, persuasive, varied language? Few fillers? |
| **Speed** (Prep & delivery) | Prep within the week's clock? Speech filled 6 min? Confident? |

## Speed clock target by week (thermometer, not furnace)
W1: 30 · W2: 26 · W3: 22 · W4: deload (hold ~22) · W5: 18 · W6: 15 · W7: 15 (+12 stretch)
*If hitting the clock forces dropping the weighing pass, the rep failed — slow down.*
