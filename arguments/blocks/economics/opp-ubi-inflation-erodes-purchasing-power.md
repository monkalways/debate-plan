Side: Opp        Motion type: Policy

CLAIM: Giving everyone more money bids up prices, so the poor's real purchasing power doesn't improve.

  L1  UBI → everyone holds more cash
      Warrant: the payment is universal and unconditional.
      Evidence: —
  L2  More cash, same short-run supply → prices rise (demand-pull)
      Warrant: buyers who can pay more raise willingness-to-pay; sellers lift prices, especially on inelastic essentials (food, housing).
      Evidence: —
  L3  Higher prices → poor's real purchasing power unchanged
      Warrant: their nominal gain is cancelled by the higher prices they now face.
      Evidence: —
